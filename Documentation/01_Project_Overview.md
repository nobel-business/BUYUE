## 1. Project Name

**Buyue Corporate Website** — a five-page bilingual (Arabic-first / English-second) marketing website for **بيوع | Buyue**, an integrated marketing and advertising agency and one of the operating brands of **Noble Business Group (مجموعة نوبل بزنس)**.

- **Public brand name (Arabic):** بيوع
- **Public brand name (Latin):** Buyue
- **Parent group:** مجموعة نوبل بزنس / Noble Business Group
- **Primary market:** Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (head office in Al-Khobar) and Egypt.
- **Working domain reference:** `buyue.com` / `buyue.sa` *(see Constraints — a domain/email inconsistency in source files must be confirmed before launch).*

## 2. Project Vision

Build a **premium, cinematic, confidence-projecting digital home** for Buyue that performs the very promise the agency sells: *that strong brand presence is not about merely appearing, but about how an audience understands, engages with, chooses, and remembers you.*

The website itself must be a **portfolio piece** — a live proof that Buyue can build a brand presence that "does not settle for competing, but imposes itself in the market" (بل يفرض نفسه في السوق). A visitor should leave with a single, unshakeable impression: *these people are serious, modern, rooted, and effective.*

The vision blends two poles that define the brand:
- **Authenticity / rootedness** — "أصيلة في جذورها" — Saudi heritage, warmth, a human voice.
- **Modern sophistication** — "متطورة في فكرها" — geometric precision, restraint, and technical polish.

---

## 3. Website Goals

1. **Communicate positioning instantly** — within the first screen, the visitor understands what Buyue is (an integrated marketing & branding agency), who it belongs to (Noble Business Group), and where it operates (KSA & Egypt).
2. **Showcase the full service journey** — from digital presence (social, campaigns, SEO, web) to physical presence (exhibition stands, outdoor, printed materials).
3. **Build credibility through proof** — surface the 30+ client logos and project galleries to establish trust fast.
4. **Convert interest into contact** — drive qualified leads to the contact form and direct channels (phone, email, social).
5. **Be self-maintainable** — allow non-technical staff to update **Services** and **Clients** (logos, projects, testimonials) via an admin panel, without a developer.
6. **Feel premium and animated** — deliver a motion-rich, cinematic experience benchmarked against the reference sites, without sacrificing performance or accessibility.
7. **Rank locally** — support the SEO metadata already written for each page (Saudi/Khobar/Egypt marketing-agency keywords).

## 5. Target Audience

Buyue sells to **decision-makers who commission marketing work.** The site must speak to several tiers at once:

**Primary audiences**
- **Marketing managers & brand owners** at mid-to-large companies (telecom, tech, F&B, real estate, retail, banking, automotive, industry).
- **Founders & executives of startups and growing brands** looking for an integrated partner rather than a single-service vendor.
- **Government & cultural entities** (الجهات الحكومية والثقافية) evaluating a credible, professional agency.

**Secondary audiences**
- **Procurement / agency-selection committees** who scan for proof, breadth, and legitimacy.
- **Potential talent & partners** assessing the agency's caliber.
- **Existing clients** returning for reference or to explore additional services.

**Audience mindset & needs**
- They are **time-poor and proof-driven** — they scan logos, scope, and results before reading prose.
- They value **clarity over cleverness** and **impact over volume** ("يُقاس بالأثر، لا بعدد المنشورات").
- They are **bilingual** — comfortable in Arabic (primary) and English (business context).
- Many browse first on **mobile**; RTL Arabic reading is the default experience.

## 8. User Journey

The journey is designed as a **narrative funnel**: capture → orient → convince → prove → convert.

**Stage 1 — Arrival (Home / الرئيسية)**
Visitor lands on the hero ("نبيع كل شيء إلا الكلام"). Within one screen they grasp the promise and see two paths: **ابدأ مشروعك معنا** (start a project) and **استعرض أعمالنا** (view our work).

**Stage 2 — Orientation (Home scroll)**
Scrolls through the services teaser (the 9-item capability list), the "لماذا تختار بيوع؟" differentiators, and the clients teaser. They now understand scope and credibility.

**Stage 3 — Deep evaluation (Services / About)**
- Interested-in-capability visitors go to **Our Services** and read the 10 detailed service blocks (each: what we do + the value).
- Trust-seeking visitors go to **About Us** for vision, mission, values, and the Noble Business Group relationship.
- Each page answers objections via its **FAQ** block.

**Stage 4 — Proof (Our Clients)**
Visitor scans the 30+ logo grid and the randomized project gallery (stands, campaigns, product shots, events, promo materials, outdoor). Credibility is confirmed.

**Stage 5 — Conversion (Contact)**
The final CTA ("جاهز تبني حضور أقوى لعلامتك؟") funnels to **Contact Us** — a short form (name, company, email, phone, service type, brief) plus direct channels (address in Khobar, phones, email, social).

**Post-conversion:** Buyue commits to a fast reply, a discovery session, and a tailored (non-templated) proposal.

```
HOME ──► SERVICES ──► CLIENTS ──► CONTACT
  │          │            │           ▲
  └── ABOUT ─┴────────────┴───────────┘
        (trust loop can enter at any point)
```

## 13. Success Criteria

**Qualitative**
- The site *feels* premium, cinematic, and unmistakably Buyue — indistinguishable in quality from a top regional agency site.
- Arabic content appears **exactly** as approved, with correct RTL rendering and no visual corruption.
- Motion enhances comprehension and delight without ever feeling gratuitous or slow.

**Quantitative (targets to validate)**
- **Performance:** Lighthouse Performance ≥ 90 on mobile; LCP < 2.5s; CLS < 0.1; INP < 200ms.
- **Accessibility:** WCAG 2.1 AA; all interactive elements keyboard-reachable; motion respects `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- **SEO:** Every page ships the approved title/description/keywords; valid semantic structure; correct `lang`/`dir` attributes.
- **Conversion:** Every page offers a clear path to Contact; contact form submits reliably and confirms to the user.
- **Maintainability:** A non-technical admin can add a service, a client logo, and a testimonial end-to-end without engineering help.
- **Cross-device:** Pixel-faithful and functional across mobile, tablet, and desktop in both AR (RTL) and EN (LTR).

**Definition of project success:** All five pages live, bilingual, animated to spec, admin-editable where required, passing the performance/accessibility/SEO bars above, with Arabic content verified verbatim against the source file.

## 14. Assumptions

1. **The Arabic content in Document 02 is final and approved.** No copywriting, paraphrasing, or "improvement" is permitted (see Document 02's binding rule).
2. **The visual identity is fixed** — colors, fonts (29LT Zarid + Articulat V3), and logo are supplied in the brand file and are not open for redesign.
3. **English is a translation of Arabic**, produced under Document 08's rules — never a literal or independent rewrite.
4. **Client logos and project images will be supplied** by Buyue (the requirement file confirms logos, project photos, and testimonials are to be added).
5. **The admin panel scope is limited** to Services and Clients management, per the requirement file — not a full CMS for all pages.
6. **The two reference sites** (azure.sa, above-limits.com) define the *animation and premium-feel benchmark*, not the layout or content to be copied.
7. **Two languages only:** Arabic (primary/source) and English (secondary).
8. **Fonts are licensed and available** for web embedding (29LT Zarid and Articulat V3) — licensing to be confirmed before build.

---

## 15. Constraints

1. **Content is locked.** Arabic copy must be reproduced verbatim; no edits, reordering of words, or punctuation changes.
2. **Brand palette is limited to 5 core colors.** Any additional tints/shades must be derived systematically (see Document 03) and stay within the identity.
3. **Bilingual + bidirectional.** Every component must work in RTL (Arabic) and LTR (English) — layout mirroring is mandatory, not optional.
4. **Motion must not compromise performance or accessibility.** Animation is subject to the performance budget and `prefers-reduced-motion`.
5. **Admin scope is Services + Clients only.** Other pages are static/managed by engineering.
6. **Data inconsistency to resolve before launch:** the website content lists the email as `info@beuyue.com`, while the brand file uses `buyue.com` / `m.nashmi@buyue.sa`. The correct email and domain **must be confirmed by the client** — do not guess. Flag, don't fix.
7. **Two phone numbers** are provided in the source (`+966 561 141 947` and a second Khobar number) — both to be confirmed and formatted consistently.
8. **No code, HTML, CSS, or framework work happens at this documentation stage** — this package precedes the build.

## 16. Future Scalability Notes

The architecture and design system should anticipate the following likely evolutions, even if they are out of scope for v1:

1. **Case studies / project detail pages** — the Clients FAQ already promises detailed case studies (dراسات حالة: goal, challenge, idea, execution, results). Design the gallery so individual projects can later expand into full case-study pages.
2. **Blog / insights section** — a content hub would reinforce SEO and thought leadership; reserve a nav slot and typographic system for long-form articles.
3. **Expanded admin/CMS** — the admin panel may later grow to manage the blog, team members, and testimonials globally; keep the data model clean and extensible.
4. **Team / careers page** — the About FAQ invites visitors to "meet our team"; a team page is a natural next addition.
5. **Additional languages/markets** — the bilingual, bidirectional foundation should make adding a third locale low-cost.
6. **Service packages / pricing** — the Services FAQ mentions both single services and integrated packages; a future packages/pricing module may be added.
7. **Regional expansion** — as Buyue grows beyond KSA & Egypt, location-specific landing pages may be needed; keep SEO metadata patterns reusable.
8. **Component reusability** — build every section as a reusable, prop-driven component (see Document 07) so new pages can be assembled from the existing system without redesign.
